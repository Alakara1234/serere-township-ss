Our History

A Message of Hope and Progress

At Serere Township Secondary School, we believe that education is the foundation for personal growth and national development. Since our establishment in 1998, we have made significant strides in academic performance, character development, and discipline. Our students continue to excel in national examinations, and we remain one of the top-performing schools in the district. This progress is the result of teamwork—dedicated teachers, eager learners, and strong community support.

We are investing in modern teaching methods, ICT, and moral guidance to prepare students for the demands of the 21st century. As we look to the future, we are filled with hope. With continued support, we aim to expand opportunities for all learners and raise a generation of responsible, skilled, and visionary citizens. — Headteacher Serere Township Secondary School
